Contact types

Contacts are the people you hand things to. Four types:

  • Staff — your own team. A staff contact can be linked to their KBM user account.
  • Contractor — vendors, maintenance, cleaners.
  • Tenant — residents who hold keys long-term.
  • Agent — outside agents borrowing lockboxes/keys for showings.

Why link checkouts to contacts

  • The contact's page shows everything they currently have and their history.
  • If the contact has an email and notifications are enabled, they get automatic checkout confirmations and overdue reminders.
  • Names stay consistent — no more "Bob", "Robert", and "bob smith" being three different people in your reports.

Adding a contact

  1. Go to Contacts → Add Contact.
  2. Name and type are required; add email and phone so notifications and receipts work.
Tip: at checkout you can pick a contact from the dropdown instead of typing a name — do this whenever the person will hold items regularly.
