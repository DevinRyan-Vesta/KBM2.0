Welcome to KBM

KBM (Keybox Manager) tracks your office's lockboxes, keys, and signs — where they are, who has them, and when they're due back. It also manages the properties they belong to, your contacts, and smart lock codes.

Logging in

  1. Open your company's KBM address in a browser.
  2. Enter your personal PIN. Every user has their own unique PIN — don't share it, since all activity is recorded under the PIN that's logged in.
For security you'll be signed out automatically after a period of inactivity (30 minutes by default). A warning appears shortly before — click anywhere to stay signed in.

Finding your way around

  • Home — the dashboard: counts of lockboxes, keys, and signs by status, plus items due back soon.
  • Inventory (Lockboxes / Keys / Signs) — full lists with search, filters, and per-item actions.
  • Operations — Check In/Out (the fast lane), Receipts, Audits, and Reports.
  • Management — Properties, Contacts, and Smart Locks.
  • Administration (admins only) — Users, Activity Logs, and Settings.

Three ways to get help

  • The ? Help button in the top bar opens the guide for the page you're on.
  • Tooltips: hover over buttons and the small ? markers for a quick explanation.
  • The Help Center link in the sidebar lists every guide (you're here now).

The 60-second tour of a typical day

  1. An agent needs a key: open Check In/Out, scan or type the key's ID, choose how many copies and who's taking them, set the expected return date, done. A receipt is offered automatically.
  2. The key comes back: same page, check it back in.
  3. End of week: open Reports to see anything overdue, and export it to Excel or PDF if needed.
