Global search

The search box in the top bar looks across everything at once: lockboxes, keys, signs, properties, contacts, smart locks, and receipts.

Using it

  • Type at least 2 characters — suggestions appear as you type, grouped by type.
  • Use ↑ / ↓ to highlight a suggestion and Enter to open it.
  • Press Enter without a selection (or click "View all results") for the full results page.
  • Esc closes the dropdown.

What matches

Search matches IDs (like KA042), labels, addresses, names, emails, phone numbers, and codes — so "Maple" finds the property, its keys, and the lockbox hanging on its door.

Tip: searching a person's name is the quickest way to answer "what does Jane still have?" — or use the by-person lookup on the Check In/Out page.
