Pieces and assembled units

Sign inventory comes in two flavors:

  • Pieces — individual components: Frames, Signs, Name Riders, Status Riders, and Bonus Riders. Each piece tracks its material and condition.
  • Assembled Units — a set of pieces built into one deployable sign. Assembling reserves the pieces so they can't be double-used.

Adding pieces

  1. Go to Signs → Add Sign.
  2. Choose subtype Piece and the piece type (Frame, Sign, rider…).
  3. For riders, enter the rider text (e.g. an agent name or "SOLD").
  4. Record material and condition so you can find good stock quickly.

Building an assembled unit

  1. Go to Signs → Sign Builder.
  2. Give the unit a label, then pick one available piece per slot.
  3. Save — the pieces are now attached to the unit. To free them up again, open the unit and disassemble it.

Deploying signs

Use Assign to place a sign at a property (long-term), or Check Out for temporary use. Both record who and where, so the Signs list always shows what's in the yard vs. what's in storage.

Tip: an assembled unit can't be deleted while it still has pieces — disassemble it first. This keeps piece inventory honest.
