The Reports page

Reports in the sidebar gathers the questions you ask most, each in a tab:

  • Overdue Returns — checkouts past their expected return date.
  • Upcoming Returns — due back in the next 30 days.
  • Long-Term Checkouts — out for more than 30 days.
  • Low Keys — keys at or below the copy threshold from Settings.
  • Checked-Out Keys — everything currently out, and with whom.
  • Property Assignments — what's assigned where.

Exporting

Each report tab has an Export ▾ button for CSV (spreadsheets), Excel (formatted workbook), or PDF (printing/email). Inventory lists (Lockboxes, Keys, Signs) have the same Export button on their own pages.

Tip: if a report is empty there's nothing to export — you'll get a note instead of a file.

Email reminders

If email notifications are enabled in Settings, KBM can send overdue reminders automatically (with a configurable grace period), plus optional confirmation emails at checkout and check-in.

Activity Logs

Admins can see the full audit trail of who did what under Administration → Activity Logs, with CSV export.
