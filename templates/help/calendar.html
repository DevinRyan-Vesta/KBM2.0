What the calendar shows

The Calendar gives you a month view of checkout activity:

  • Due back — items whose expected return date falls on that day.
  • Checked out — items that went out that day.
  • Overdue — anything past its return date is flagged in red, both on its due day and in the overdue list at the top.

Getting around

  • Use ← / → to move between months and Today to jump back.
  • Click any item on a day to open its detail page.
Tip: the calendar is a planning view — the checkout data comes from the same records as the Reports page, so anything overdue here also appears in the Overdue Returns report (and in overdue reminder emails, if enabled).
