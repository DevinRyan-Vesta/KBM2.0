Who can change settings

Settings apply to your whole company and are editable by admins and owners only, under Administration → Settings.

Email notifications

  • Master switch — turn all email on or off.
  • On checkout / on check-in — confirmation emails to the contact involved (they need an email address on their contact record).
  • Overdue reminders — automatic emails when an expected return date passes.
  • Grace period — days past the return date before the first overdue reminder goes out (0 = same day).

Thresholds

  • Low keys threshold — keys with this many total copies or fewer get the red low-stock marker and appear in the Low Keys report. Default 4.
  • Default checkout days — pre-fills the expected return date at checkout (default 7). You can always override per checkout.

Receipts

Receipt header and footer are free text printed on every receipt — company name and phone up top, return policy at the bottom.

Tip: settings take effect immediately — no restart needed.
