What receipts are

Every checkout creates a receipt: who took what, how many, when, why, and when it's due back. Receipts are the paper trail for anything that leaves the office.

Printing at checkout

Right after a checkout, KBM asks if you want to print a receipt — it opens print-ready in a new tab.

Finding a receipt later

  1. Open Receipts in the sidebar.
  2. Search by the person's name, the item, or browse recent checkouts.
  3. Open the receipt and print it, or re-send it as needed.

Customizing the receipt text

Admins can set a header (e.g. your company name and phone number) and a footer (e.g. return policy) that print on every receipt — see Settings → Receipts.

Exporting

The receipts list has an Export option for a CSV of checkout history — useful for audits or end-of-month reviews.
